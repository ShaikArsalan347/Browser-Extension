About Us

Tab Time Tracker is a user-friendly browser extension designed to help you monitor and manage your online activity more efficiently. With a sleek and intuitive interface, this extension provides a detailed breakdown of the time you spend on each website throughout the day, helping you better understand and optimize your browsing habits. Whether you're a student trying to focus on studies, a professional aiming to stay productive, or simply someone curious about your online patterns, Tab Time Tracker offers powerful insights into your daily browsing.
