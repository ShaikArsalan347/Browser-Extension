Reclaim Your Day: Understanding Your Digital Habits

Published on Oct 24, 2024 • 5 min read

In an era where the line between "productive work" and "mindless scrolling" is thinner than ever, staying conscious of where your time goes is a superpower.

The "Internet Black Hole"

Most of us spend hours every day in a browser. Without a mirror to show us our actual habits, it’s easy to overestimate our productivity and underestimate our distractions.

Why Track Your Time?

  • Eliminate Fatigue: Identify when you are context switching too often.
  • Privacy First: Your data stays on your device, always.
  • Visual Charts: Turn raw numbers into easy-to-read pie charts.

How Tab Time Tracker Helps
